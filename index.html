About

Congratulations! You found my personal website! I am a PhD student in the PPLV group at the Computer Science Department of University College London, supervised by Alexandra Silva and Jurriaan Rot. I am interested in the various flavours of automata and their Kleene theorems, as well as the interaction between algebra and coalgebra in computer science more generally. The following is a picture of me in a Frodo costume at 11 years old.

When I'm not doing math, I am ideally either (a) making or eating bread, (b) fostering rescue dogs, or (c) making ambient music, or (d) reading poetry (someday I will add bouldering to this list, but bouldering is hard and so is finding motivation sometimes). I also keep a very shameful collection of internet rabbit holes and strange websites, and really like weird movies.
