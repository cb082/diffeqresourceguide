About

Congratulations! You've found the personal website of Todd Schmid! Todd is a PhD student in the PPLV group at the Computer Science Department of University College London, working under Alexandra Silva and Jurrian Rot. Todd is interested in all the various flavours of automata and their Kleene theorems, as well as the interaction between algebra and coalgebra in computer science more generally. The following is a picture of Todd in a Frodo costume at 11 years old.

When Todd isn't drawing graphs and diagrams, he likes to make/eat bread, foster rescue dogs, and make ambient music. He also keeps a collection of internet rabbit holes and likes to host weird-movie nights when he can.
