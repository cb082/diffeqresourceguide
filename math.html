Interests/Past Work

I am generally interested in algebra, category theory, and mathematical logic, and the influence these fields have had on the theory of machines. I am officially a part of the ReLiC project, whose aim is to find a coalgebraic semantics for reductive logic applicable to proof search. More detailed descriptions of the various projects I am involved with can be found above.
